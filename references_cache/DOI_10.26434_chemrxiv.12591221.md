# Extension of Roles in the ChEBI Ontology
**Authors:** Charles Tapley Hoyt, Christopher Mungall, Nicole Vasilevsky, Daniel Domingo-Fernández, Matthew Healy, Viswa Colluru
**DOI:** [10.26434/chemrxiv.12591221](https://doi.org/10.26434/chemrxiv.12591221)

## Content

This manuscript describes the extension of the ChEBI ontology to connect its roles to their targets in other controlled vocabularies, to improve interoperability, and to enable inference.
