# Prototype semantic infrastructure for automated small molecule classification and annotation in lipidomics
**Authors:** Leonid L Chepelev, Alexandre Riazanov, Alexandre Kouznetsov, Hong Sang Low, Michel Dumontier, Christopher JO Baker

**Journal:** BMC Bioinformatics (2011)

**DOI:** [10.1186/1471-2105-12-303](https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2105-12-303)

The formal lipid ontology used in this work extends a part of the LiPrO ontology and
draws on the lipid hierarchy in the LIPID MAPS database. The authors present a prototype
semantic infrastructure of formally defined, machine-readable ontologies and reasoning
services (semantic web services) for automated, structure-based classification and
annotation of small molecules in lipidomics.
