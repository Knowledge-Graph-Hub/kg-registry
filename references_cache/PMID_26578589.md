ORegAnno 3.0: a community-driven resource for curated regulatory annotation.

The Open Regulatory Annotation database (ORegAnno) is a resource for curated
regulatory annotation. It contains information about regulatory regions,
transcription factor binding sites, RNA binding sites, regulatory variants,
haplotypes, and other regulatory elements, manually curated from the scientific
literature by a community of expert curators. ORegAnno 3.0 records are linked to
supporting publications, experimental evidence, target genes, and source species,
and the data are integrated with and distributed through the UCSC Genome Browser
and Ensembl.
