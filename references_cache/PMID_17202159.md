# TRED: a transcriptional regulatory element database, new entries and other development
**Authors:** C. Jiang, Z. Xuan, F. Zhao, M. Q. Zhang
**Journal:** Nucleic Acids Res 2007;35(Database issue):D137-D140
**DOI:** [10.1093/nar/gkl1041](https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkl1041)
**PMID:** [17202159](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/17202159/)

## Content

TRED (Transcriptional Regulatory Element Database) is a curated database of cis-regulatory elements, emphasizing transcription factor binding sites, transcription factor to promoter regulatory interactions, and promoter annotations for human, mouse, and rat. It combines computational promoter prediction with literature-based manual curation. This paper describes new entries and other developments of the resource, originally hosted at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory.
