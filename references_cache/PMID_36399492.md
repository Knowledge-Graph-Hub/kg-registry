The European Nucleotide Archive (ENA; https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena), maintained by 
the European Molecular Biology Laboratory's European Bioinformatics Institute 
(EMBL-EBI), offers those producing data an open and supported platform for the 
management, archiving, publication, and dissemination of data; and to the 
scientific community as a whole, it offers a globally comprehensive data set 
through a host of data discovery and retrieval tools. Here, we describe recent 
updates to the ENA's submission and retrieval services as well as focused 
efforts to improve connectivity, reusability, and interoperability of ENA data 
and metadata.
