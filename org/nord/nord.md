The National Organization for Rare Disorders (NORD) is a unique patient advocacy organization dedicated to individuals with rare diseases and the organizations that serve them. Founded in 1983, NORD provides crucial services including comprehensive disease information, patient assistance programs, research funding, and policy advocacy for the rare disease community.
