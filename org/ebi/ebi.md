The European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI) is an international research organization and the world's leading provider of freely accessible biological data and bioinformatics services. As part of the European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL), EBI hosts and maintains over 30 major databases covering all aspects of molecular biology, including genomics, proteomics, metabolomics, structural biology, systems biology, and chemical biology. EBI provides not only data storage but also sophisticated computational tools for data analysis and integration, serving millions of researchers worldwide.
