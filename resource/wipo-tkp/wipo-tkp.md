## Description

The **WIPO Traditional Knowledge Resources** are a set of web pages and linked
databases maintained by the **World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO)**
covering traditional knowledge (TK), traditional cultural expressions, and
genetic resources. The portal aggregates policy documents, guidance, and
pointers to registries and databases that document traditional and herbal
medicine knowledge held by indigenous and local communities.

This documentation of traditional medicinal systems is relevant to pharmacology
and natural product research, and the WIPO TK resources have been cited as an
upstream source for **PharmDB**, a database of traditional Korean medicine. The
portal functions as a reference hub linking to dispersed materials rather than
providing a single downloadable dataset.
