# Aunt Bertha (findhelp)

Aunt Bertha, now branded as findhelp, is a social care network that maintains a
searchable directory of free and reduced-cost social services across the United
States. Records include service names, descriptions, contact details, physical
locations, languages served, cost and eligibility, availability, hours, and
service categories.

DREAM-KG uses Aunt Bertha as the primary directory of Philadelphia social
services, populating the knowledge graph's service, organization, location, and
hours entities.
