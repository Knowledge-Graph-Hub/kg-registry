# Proteopedia

Proteopedia is a free, collaborative 3D encyclopedia of proteins and other biomolecules,
hosted at the Weizmann Institute of Science. Each page presents macromolecular structure
and function together, with interactive 3D scenes (powered by Jmol/JSmol) linked directly
to descriptive text.

Content and features include:

- Structure pages for entries in the Protein Data Bank
- Curated, educational pages on proteins, drugs, and biological topics
- Interactive 3D visualization tied to the surrounding narrative

GeneCards integrates structure and function information from Proteopedia.
