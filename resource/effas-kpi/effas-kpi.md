KPIs for ESG is a standardized framework of Key Performance Indicators for
Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) reporting, published by EFFAS together
with DVFA. The "KPIs for ESG 3.0" edition defines sector-specific ESG indicators
aligned with the ICB classification and provides the KPI vocabulary used by
EmeraldGraph/EmeraldMind.
