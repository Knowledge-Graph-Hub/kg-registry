# IMGT

IMGT, the international ImMunoGeneTics information system, is the global reference for
immunogenetics and immunoinformatics. Created in 1989 at Montpellier, France, IMGT
provides a standardized, high-quality view of the immunoglobulins (antibodies), T cell
receptors, major histocompatibility complex molecules, and related proteins of the
immune system in human and other vertebrate species.

IMGT comprises:

- Sequence, gene, and structure databases (including IMGT/GENE-DB and IMGT/3Dstructure-DB)
- Analysis tools such as IMGT/V-QUEST and IMGT/HighV-QUEST
- Reference directories and the IMGT-ONTOLOGY controlled vocabulary

GeneCards integrates immunogenetics data from IMGT.
