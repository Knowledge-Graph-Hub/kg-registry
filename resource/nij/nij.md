# National Institute of Justice

## Overview

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is the research, development, and evaluation
agency of the U.S. Department of Justice within the Office of Justice Programs. NIJ
sponsors and disseminates research on crime, justice, victimization, firearms violence,
forensic science, and public safety.

## Role in the Open Knowledge Network

NIJ is a partner agency in the NSF Prototype Open Knowledge Network (Proto-OKN)
program. It contributed the "Data on Nonfatal Firearms Injuries" use case that
motivated the Neighborhood Information KG (NIKG), which integrates criminal
victimization data with neighborhood-level demographic and environmental context.
