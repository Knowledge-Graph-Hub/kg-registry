## Description

The **National Survey on Drug Use and Health (NSDUH)** is SAMHSA's annual
national survey on tobacco, alcohol, and illicit drug use, substance use
disorders, and mental health among the US civilian, noninstitutionalized
population.

In the KG-Registry, NSDUH is recorded as an upstream data source for the
**Rural Resilience KG**, whose substance-use categories and survey variables
are extracted from NSDUH.

## Products

- **SAMHSA NSDUH data page** — the official landing page for NSDUH
  documentation, methodology, and public-use data files.
